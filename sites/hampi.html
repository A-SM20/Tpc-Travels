Welcome to Hampi

Explore the ancient ruins of the Vijayanagara Empire

Must-Visit Places

Virupaksha Temple

Ancient temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, featuring intricate carvings and a towering gopuram.

Vittala Temple

Famous for its musical pillars and the iconic stone chariot, this temple showcases exceptional architecture.

Hemakuta Hill

Offers panoramic views of Hampi and is dotted with ancient temples and boulder formations.

Things to Do

Rock Climbing

Experience the thrill of climbing the unique boulder formations that dot the landscape.

Photography

Capture the stunning architecture and natural beauty of this UNESCO World Heritage site.

Cycling

Explore the ruins and surrounding areas on a bicycle for a unique experience.

Local Delicacies

Paddu

Little dumpling-like balls made from Dosa batter

Bisi Bele Bath

A traditional Karnataka rice dish with lentils and vegetables.

Khaara Mandakki

Puffed rice, thin poha mixed with mixture, hint of garlic, and mild chilly powder.

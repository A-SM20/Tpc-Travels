Welcome to Mysore

Experience the grandeur of the City of Palaces

Must-Visit Places

Mysore Palace

Magnificent royal palace featuring Indo-Saracenic architecture and stunning interiors.

Chamundi Hills

Sacred hill with ancient temple and panoramic views of the city.

Brindavan Gardens

Beautiful gardens with musical fountains and scenic beauty.

Things to Do

Palace Tours

Explore the rich history and architecture of Mysore's royal palaces.

Shopping

Shop for silk sarees, sandalwood products, and traditional crafts.

Art & Culture

Experience traditional dance performances and art exhibitions.

Local Delicacies

Mysore Pak

Traditional sweet made from ghee, sugar, and gram flour.

Mysore Biryani

Fragrant rice dish with aromatic spices and tender meat.

Bisbelabath

It is a spicy porridge-like dish basically made of Lentil rice cooked with tomatoes and spices.

Welcome to Pondicherry

Experience the perfect blend of French colonial charm and Indian culture

Must-Visit Places

Rock Beach

A scenic promenade along the Bay of Bengal, perfect for evening walks and sunrise views.

Sacred Heart Basilica

A stunning neo-Gothic church with beautiful stained glass windows and French colonial architecture.

Auroville

An experimental township dedicated to human unity and sustainable living.

Things to Do

Beach Activities

Enjoy water sports, beach yoga, and sunset views at Paradise Beach.

French Quarter Tour

Cycle through the colonial streets and admire French architecture.

Wellness & Yoga

Experience traditional yoga and meditation at various ashrams.

Local Delicacies

French Croissants

Freshly baked buttery croissants from French bakeries.

Pondy Moussaka

Originally from Greece, Moussake is a layered flavorful side dish made with fried eggplants and potatoes.

Kadugu Yerra

Rich and aromatic prawns cooked in a spicy and tangy tomato sauce with potatoes, vinegar, fenugreek, mustard paste and creamy coconut milk.

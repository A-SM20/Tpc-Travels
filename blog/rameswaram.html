Welcome to Rameshwaram

The Sacred Island of Tamil Nadu

Must-Visit Places

Ramanathaswamy Temple

One of the twelve Jyotirlinga temples, known for its magnificent architecture and sacred waters.

Dhanushkodi Beach

A ghost town turned tourist spot, where the Bay of Bengal meets the Indian Ocean.

Gandamadana Parvatham

The site is believed to bear the footprints of Lord Rama on a chakra.

Things to Do

Holy Dip

Take a sacred bath in the holy waters of Agni Theertham.

Beach Activities

Enjoy water sports and beach activities at Dhanushkodi.

Temple Visit

Experience the spiritual atmosphere of Ramanathaswamy Temple.

Local Delicacies

Puliyodarai

Its a dish made by mixing rice and tamarind. It is often served as prasadam to God before human consumption.

Fish Curry

Fresh seafood prepared in traditional Tamil style with aromatic spices.

Rameshwaram Podi Idli

Soft, fluffy idlis served with podi on top.
